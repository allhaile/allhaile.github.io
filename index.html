About

Haile is a recent college graduate from the University of California, Berkeley and studied Cognitive Science. With her major she is able to combine her passion for design with the main principles of cognitive science in order to create her technical projects. Her technical interets are full stack web development, user experience/interface design, and most recently data science. Haile's dream is become a technical evangelist.

Haile is passionate about learning something new everyday and understands that learning is a privilege. She is always questioning ways to contribute back into her community, more specifically to other Black women in technology. Haile believes it's important to not only bring learning but also awareness and opportunities. She understands how everyone does not have equal access to the same resources and is passionate about leveling the playing field. With this she is an active Butterfly of The Butterfly Movement, and has leveraged her technical skills to assist with branding efforts of the organization.

In high school she was a scholar in SMASH Academy. SMASH Academy is a three-year STEM residential college prep program for students of color. During this program she was introduced to utilizing technology for social good, mentorship with industry professionals and public speaking training.
