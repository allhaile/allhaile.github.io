About

Haile is a graduate of University of California, Berkeley with a major in Cognitive Science. With her major she is able to combine her passion for design with the main principles of cognitive science in order to create her technical projects. Her technical interets are full stack web development, user experience/interface design, and most recently data science.

Haile is passionate about learning and finding new ways to contribute to her community of other Black Women in technology. She is an active Butterfly of The Butterfly Movement, and has leveraged her technical skills to assist with branding efforts of the organization.

In high school she was a scholar in SMASH Academy. SMASH Academy is a three-year STEM residential college prep program for students of color. During this program she was introduced to utilizing technology for social good, mentorship with industry professionals and public speaking training.
